Agave is a construction software platform that integrates field and finance systems, enabling companies to synchronize data between project management tools like Autodesk Build and financial systems like Deltek ComputerEase. The company offers automated solutions for invoice processing, expense management, and financial analytics, helping contractors streamline workflows and improve data accuracy across their operations.
